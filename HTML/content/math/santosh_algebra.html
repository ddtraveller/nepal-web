Profit and Loss in Math

Profit = Selling Price - Cost Price

Profit% = (Profit / Cost Price) × 100

Loss = Cost Price - Selling Price

Loss% = (Loss / Cost Price) × 100

SP = CP + Profit

SP = CP - Loss

Vision Statement: Project Possible

Empowering and supporting migrant families in Kathmandu through education, health, and early childhood development.

Understanding Nepal's Internal Migration

  • Migrants: From Bajura & Kalikot, mostly farmers and marginalized communities
  • Push Factors: Poor education, healthcare, agriculture, climate change

Regional Context

Bajura District: Poorest (HDI 0.364)

Kalikot District: Second poorest (HDI 0.374)

Initiative 1: Educational Support Program

  • Supports 100 children
  • Provides uniforms, shoes, backpacks, bottles, stationery

Initiative 2: Women's Health Support

  • Hygiene kits and menstrual health education for 200+ women monthly
  • Pap smears and awareness events

Initiative 3: Early Childhood Development Center

  • Safe space for children 2–5 years old
  • English program, learning tools, meals, parent sessions

Long-Term Impact

  • Breaks poverty cycle
  • Improves health & education outcomes
  • Preserves culture while helping urban integration
