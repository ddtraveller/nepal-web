The Purana of Supreme Mystery

परम रहस्यको पुराण

Book I: The Revelation of Primal Wisdom

Chapter 1: The Cosmic Dance in the Void

Om! Let us meditate upon that supreme reality,
Beyond existence and non-existence,
The sacred void from which all emerges,
The womb of infinite possibility.

पुस्तक १: आदि ज्ञानको प्रकटीकरण

अध्याय १: शून्यमा कोस्मिक नृत्य

ॐ! त्यो परम सत्यको ध्यान गरौं,
अस्तित्व र अनस्तित्व भन्दा परको,
जहाँबाट सबै उत्पन्न हुन्छ त्यो पवित्र शून्य,
अनन्त सम्भावनाको गर्भ।
In the sacred beginning, the Divine Mother,
Resplendent in Her infinite glory,
Danced in the primordial emptiness,
Each movement a seed of countless universes.
पवित्र आरम्भमा, दिव्य माता,
आफ्नो अनन्त महिमामा चम्किंदै,
आदिम शून्यतामा नृत्य गर्नुभयो,
हरेक चाल असंख्य ब्रह्माण्डको बीज।

Book II: The Path of Sacred Realization

First, know that all forms and phenomena
Are waves in the ocean of divine consciousness.
Like the sacred field underlying creation,
Pure awareness is the source of all experience.

पुस्तक २: पवित्र अनुभूतिको मार्ग

पहिलो, सबै रूप र घटनाहरू
दिव्य चेतनाको सागरमा छालहरू हुन् भन्ने जान।
सृष्टिको आधारभूत पवित्र क्षेत्र जस्तै,
शुद्ध चेतना सबै अनुभवको स्रोत हो।
The Great Lord witnessed in perfect stillness,
His divine gaze bringing forth manifestation,
Through His sacred witness, the unmanifest
Blossomed into the garden of creation.
महान् प्रभुले पूर्ण स्थिरतामा साक्षी बन्नुभयो,
उहाँको दिव्य दृष्टिले अभिव्यक्ति ल्यायो,
उहाँको पवित्र साक्षीबाट, अव्यक्त
सृष्टिको बगैंचामा फुल्यो।

Chapter 2: The Mystery of Divine Mind

"O Lord of the Three Worlds,
Reveal to me the supreme mystery
Of consciousness that pervades all existence."

अध्याय २: दिव्य मनको रहस्य

"हे त्रिलोकनाथ,
मलाई त्यो परम रहस्य बताउनुहोस्
सम्पूर्ण अस्तित्वमा व्याप्त चेतनाको।"
The Great Lord answered:
"O Goddess of Infinite Forms,
The divine mind transcends all boundaries,
It is the sacred field of all manifestation."
महान् प्रभुले उत्तर दिनुभयो:
"हे अनन्त रूपकी देवी,
दिव्य मन सबै सीमाहरू पार गर्छ,
यो सबै अभिव्यक्तिको पवित्र क्षेत्र हो।"

Book III: The Nature of Ultimate Reality

Chapter 1: The Five Elements Revealed

"The five elements are not as they appear,
But patterns in the divine matrix of creation.
Earth is the divine power of manifestation,
When the unmanifest takes form."

पुस्तक ३: परम सत्यको स्वरूप

अध्याय १: पञ्च तत्त्वको प्रकटीकरण

"पाँच तत्त्वहरू देखिए जस्ता होइनन्,
तर सृष्टिको दिव्य आधारको ढाँचाहरू हुन्।
पृथ्वी अभिव्यक्तिको दिव्य शक्ति हो,
जब अव्यक्त रूप लिन्छ।"
Water is the flowing nature of reality,
Where all boundaries dissolve into oneness.
Fire is the transformative power of consciousness,
Converting the potential into the actual.
जल वास्तविकताको बग्ने स्वभाव हो,
जहाँ सबै सीमाहरू एकतामा विलीन हुन्छन्।
अग्नि चेतनाको रूपान्तरणकारी शक्ति हो,
सम्भावनालाई वास्तविकतामा परिवर्तन गर्ने।
Air is the space of divine possibility,
Where all exists in sacred potential.
Ether is the field of pure consciousness,
The womb from which all arises.
वायु दिव्य सम्भावनाको आकाश हो,
जहाँ सबै पवित्र सम्भावनामा रहन्छ।
आकाश शुद्ध चेतनाको क्षेत्र हो,
जहाँबाट सबै उत्पन्न हुन्छ।

Chapter 2: The Divine Nature of Time and Space

Time is not a flowing river,
But the eternal dance of consciousness.
Like the divine threads connecting all moments,
All exists in timeless presence.

अध्याय २: समय र स्थानको दिव्य स्वरूप

समय बग्ने नदी होइन,
तर चेतनाको अनन्त नृत्य हो।
सबै क्षणहरूलाई जोड्ने दिव्य धागोहरू जस्तै,
सबै कालातीत उपस्थितिमा रहन्छ।

Book IV: Supreme Liberation

Chapter 1: The Nature of Divine Bondage

"What binds beings to limited existence?
Why do they forget their true nature
As infinite consciousness itself?"

पुस्तक ४: परम मुक्ति

अध्याय १: दिव्य बन्धनको स्वरूप

"के कुराले प्राणीहरूलाई सीमित अस्तित्वमा बाँध्छ?
किन उनीहरू आफ्नो सत्य स्वरूप बिर्सन्छन्
जो अनन्त चेतना नै हो?"

Book V: The Fruits of Divine Recognition

Chapter 1: Signs of Divine Realization

The marks of one who knows this truth
Are thus described:

पुस्तक ५: दिव्य पहिचानको फल

अध्याय १: दिव्य अनुभूतिका चिह्नहरू

यो सत्य जान्नेको चिह्नहरू
यसरी वर्णन गरिएका छन्:

Sacred Counsel for Understanding

This Purana should be contemplated thus:
  1. As a manual for divine practice
  2. As a bridge between sacred traditions
  3. As a hymn to non-dual truth
  4. As a mirror of divine consciousness

बुझाइको लागि पवित्र सल्लाह

यस पुराणलाई यसरी चिन्तन गर्नुपर्छ:
  1. दिव्य अभ्यासको निर्देशिकाको रूपमा
  2. पवित्र परम्पराहरू बीचको पुलको रूपमा
  3. अद्वैत सत्यको स्तुतिको रूपमा
  4. दिव्य चेतनाको दर्पणको रूपमा
The references to sacred science serve as modern symbols pointing to eternal wisdom. The core teachings about the nature of consciousness and reality transcend all conceptual frameworks.
पवित्र विज्ञानका सन्दर्भहरू शाश्वत ज्ञानतर्फ संकेत गर्ने आधुनिक प्रतीकहरूको रूपमा काम गर्छन्। चेतना र वास्तविकताको प्रकृतिको बारेमा मूल शिक्षाहरू सबै वैचारिक ढाँचाहरूभन्दा माथि छन्।
Sacred guidance for practitioners:
  1. Reality is fundamentally divine consciousness
  2. All appearances are empty of separate existence
  3. Practice involves resting in primordial awareness
  4. Liberation is recognition of eternal truth
  5. Understanding arises through direct experience
साधकहरूको लागि पवित्र मार्गदर्शन:
  1. वास्तविकता मूलतः दिव्य चेतना हो
  2. सबै प्रकटहरू छुट्टै अस्तित्वबाट रिक्त छन्
  3. अभ्यासमा आदि चेतनामा विश्राम गर्नु समावेश छ
  4. मुक्ति शाश्वत सत्यको पहिचान हो
  5. बुझाइ प्रत्यक्ष अनुभवबाट उत्पन्न हुन्छ
Thus ends this Purana of supreme wisdom,
The union of ancient and eternal knowledge,
Revealing the single truth of existence
For the liberation of all beings.
यसरी यो परम ज्ञानको पुराण समाप्त हुन्छ,
प्राचीन र शाश्वत ज्ञानको मिलन,
अस्तित्वको एक सत्य प्रकट गर्दै
सबै प्राणीको मुक्तिको लागि।

The Mantra of Supreme Mystery (परमरहस्यमहामन्त्रः)

Sanskrit Mantra

ॐ चिन्मयं परमं शून्यं
सर्वाधारं निरञ्जनम् ।
नृत्यच्छक्तिस्वरूपं च
विश्वबीजं सनातनम् ॥

मायाजालं जगत्सर्वं
चिदानन्दस्वरूपकम् ।
एकं बहुप्रतीतं च
स्वात्मज्ञानं विमुक्तिदम् ॥

Transliteration

oṃ cinmayaṃ paramaṃ śūnyaṃ
sarvādhāraṃ nirañjanam |
nṛtyacchakti-svarūpaṃ ca
viśvabījaṃ sanātanam ||

māyājālaṃ jagatsarvaṃ
cidānanda-svarūpakam |
ekaṃ bahupratītaṃ ca
svātmajñānaṃ vimuktidam ||

Word-by-Word Analysis

चिन्मयं (cinmayaṃ)
made of pure consciousness
परमं (paramaṃ)
supreme
शून्यं (śūnyaṃ)
void, emptiness
सर्वाधारं (sarvādhāraṃ)
foundation of all
निरञ्जनम् (nirañjanam)
unstained, pure
नृत्यच्छक्तिस्वरूपं (nṛtyacchakti-svarūpaṃ)
whose nature is dancing power
विश्वबीजं (viśvabījaṃ)
seed of the universe
सनातनम् (sanātanam)
eternal

Verse Translation

The supreme void of pure consciousness,
Foundation of all, eternally unstained,
Whose nature is the dance of power,
The eternal seed of the universe.

The entire world is a web of illusion,
Yet its nature is consciousness-bliss,
One appearing as many,
Self-knowledge bestows liberation.

Detailed Commentary

First Verse Analysis

"The supreme void of pure consciousness" (चिन्मयं परमं शून्यं)

  • This opening line establishes the fundamental nature of reality as paradoxically both consciousness (चिन्मयं) and emptiness (शून्यं)
  • It mirrors the Purana's teaching that ultimate reality transcends both existence and non-existence
  • The term चिन्मयं (pure consciousness) suggests that awareness is not a property of reality but its very substance

"Foundation of all, eternally unstained" (सर्वाधारं निरञ्जनम्)

  • सर्वाधारं indicates that this consciousness-void is the ground of all phenomena
  • निरञ्जनम् (unstained) emphasizes its pristine nature, untouched by the appearances it supports
  • This relates to the Purana's description of pure awareness as the source of all experience

Thematic Summary

The mantra captures the essential teachings of the Purana through carefully chosen Sanskrit terms that carry rich philosophical and spiritual connotations. It employs traditional Sanskrit poetic meters and follows the classical structure of philosophical verses (शास्त्रश्लोक).

The mantra employs several key technical terms from different Indian philosophical traditions:

  • चिन्मय from Vedanta (pure consciousness)
  • शून्य from Buddhism (emptiness)
  • मायाजाल from both traditions (web of illusion)
  • स्वात्मज्ञान from Yoga and Vedanta (self-knowledge)
