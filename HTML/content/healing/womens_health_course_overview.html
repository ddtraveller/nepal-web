Women's Health and Wellness Course

Course Overview

This comprehensive three-day course is designed to empower women with knowledge and practical skills for managing their health throughout different life stages. From understanding menstrual wellness to pregnancy care and beyond, participants will gain valuable insights into women's health and self-care practices.

Note: This course is open to all women and individuals interested in women's health. The content is designed to be inclusive, respectful, and evidence-based. Medical professionals will be present to answer questions, but this course does not replace medical advice or care.
