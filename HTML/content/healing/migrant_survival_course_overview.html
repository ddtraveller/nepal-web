Migrant Survival and Self-Sufficiency Course

Course Overview

This three-day intensive course teaches essential survival skills and self-sufficiency techniques for migrants facing challenging situations. The focus is on practical skills for finding safe shelter, accessing basic necessities, and maintaining communication with family while traveling with limited resources.

Emergency Resources

  • Free emergency numbers work without SIM card: 112 (most countries)
  • Nepal Embassy emergency contacts (varies by country)
  • Local Nepali community group contacts
  • Free medical clinic locations

Safety Notes

  • Always prioritize personal safety
  • Know local emergency numbers

  • Keep important documents safe
  • Share location with trusted contacts
