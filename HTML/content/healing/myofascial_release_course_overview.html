Myofascial Release Course

Course Overview

This comprehensive three-day certification course provides in-depth training in myofascial release techniques and myofascial unwinding. Participants will learn to assess fascial restrictions, apply appropriate release techniques, and facilitate the body's natural unwinding process. The course combines detailed anatomical study with extensive hands-on practice to develop the palpation skills, listening touch, and technical expertise needed for effective myofascial treatment and unwinding facilitation.

Prerequisites: This course is designed for licensed healthcare professionals including physical therapists, occupational therapists, massage therapists, athletic trainers, and other manual therapy practitioners. Strong foundation in musculoskeletal anatomy and basic palpation skills required.

Learning Outcomes

Upon completion of this course, participants will be able to:

  • Understand fascial anatomy and its role in body function
  • Assess fascial restrictions throughout the body
  • Apply appropriate MFR techniques for different body regions
  • Develop and utilize listening touch for unwinding
  • Facilitate whole-body unwinding processes
  • Guide regional and segmental unwinding
  • Understand the emotional aspects of unwinding
  • Support trauma release through unwinding
  • Integrate direct and indirect techniques
  • Create safe space for spontaneous movement
  • Document treatment progress effectively
