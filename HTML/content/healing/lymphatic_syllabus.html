Course Objectives

Upon successful completion of this course, participants will be able to:

  • Demonstrate comprehensive understanding of lymphatic system anatomy and physiology
  • Perform complete MLD sequences for face, neck, upper extremities, trunk, and lower extremities
  • Conduct thorough lymphedema assessments and measurements
  • Create appropriate treatment plans for various clinical conditions
  • Document treatments and track client progress effectively
  • Apply MLD techniques safely for post-surgical cases
  • Perform multi-layer compression bandaging for lymphedema management
  • Teach appropriate lymphedema exercises and self-management techniques
  • Implement comprehensive CLD treatment protocols
  • Identify contraindications and special considerations for MLD and CLD

Assessment Criteria

  • Attendance and Participation (20%)

    • Active participation in discussions and workshops
  • Practical Skills Assessment (60%)

    • Treatment planning
