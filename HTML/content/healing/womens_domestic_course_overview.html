Women's Safety and Crisis Response Course

Course Overview

This one-day intensive course provides practical guidance for women facing domestic violence in Nepal. The focus is on immediate safety planning, crisis response, and understanding available legal and support options. All information is provided in both Nepali and English.

Key Topics Covered

  • Immediate Safety Planning:
    • Creating a code word with trusted neighbors
    • Identifying safe rooms and escape routes
    • Preparing emergency bags with essentials
    • Teaching children emergency procedures
  • Crisis Response:
    • De-escalation strategies during violent episodes
    • Protecting yourself and children
    • When and how to contact police
    • Accessing emergency shelters
  • Legal Rights:
    • Understanding the Domestic Violence Act
    • Filing police complaints
    • Obtaining protection orders
    • Child custody rights
  • Financial Protection:
    • Securing important documents
    • Opening separate bank accounts
    • Building emergency funds
    • Accessing financial support

Important Safety Notes

  • Keep all course materials in a safe, hidden location
  • Use private browsing when accessing online resources
  • Save emergency numbers under alternate names
  • Consider using a friend's address for correspondence
