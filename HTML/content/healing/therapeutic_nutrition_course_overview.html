Therapeutic Nutrition and Holistic Healing Course

Course Overview

This comprehensive three-day course combines modern nutritional science with traditional healing practices to provide a holistic approach to health and healing. Participants will learn practical strategies for using food as medicine, understanding detoxification processes, and implementing ancient healing wisdom for modern health challenges.

Note: This course provides educational information and should be used in conjunction with, not as a replacement for, professional medical care. Participants should consult their healthcare providers before making significant dietary changes, especially when managing specific medical conditions.
