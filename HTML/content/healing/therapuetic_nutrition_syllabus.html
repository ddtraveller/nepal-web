Therapeutic Nutrition and Holistic Healing Course - Syllabus

Course Information

Duration: 3 days (21 hours total)
Format: Lectures, workshops, and hands-on practice
Instructors: Nutritionist, Ayurvedic practitioner, holistic health specialist

Important Notice

This course is educational in nature and does not qualify participants to diagnose or treat medical conditions. All participants should:

  • Consult healthcare providers before implementing major dietary changes
  • Maintain ongoing medical care for existing conditions
  • Understand that this information complements but does not replace medical treatment

Course Objectives

Upon completion, participants will be able to:

  • Understand fundamental principles of therapeutic nutrition
  • Identify and select healing foods for various conditions
  • Apply safe detoxification principles
  • Understand and apply basic Ayurvedic concepts
  • Create therapeutic meal plans
  • Use herbs and spices therapeutically
  • Support recovery through nutrition
  • Implement organic food selection strategies
