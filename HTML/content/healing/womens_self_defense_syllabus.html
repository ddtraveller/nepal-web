Course Description

This intensive three-day course empowers women with practical personal safety and self-defense skills. The curriculum integrates awareness training, verbal defense strategies, and physical techniques appropriate for various situations. The course emphasizes prevention, awareness, and confidence-building while providing realistic, effective self-defense methods.

Course Objectives

Upon successful completion of this course, participants will be able to:

  • Implement effective personal safety strategies in daily life
  • Recognize and respond to potential threats before they escalate
  • Apply verbal de-escalation techniques effectively
  • Execute basic physical self-defense techniques properly
  • Access and utilize community safety resources
  • Develop and maintain personal safety plans

Course Structure and Content

Day 1: Awareness and Prevention

  • Personal safety principles and mindset
  • Situational awareness training
  • Verbal defense and boundary setting
  • Safety planning and risk assessment

Day 2: Physical Defense Fundamentals

  • Defensive stance and movement
  • Strike techniques and target areas
  • Release from grabs and holds
  • Ground defense basics

Day 3: Integration and Practical Application

  • Scenario-based training
  • Multiple opponent awareness
  • Environmental defense strategies
  • Ongoing practice methods

Assessment Criteria

This is a pass/fail course. To receive a certificate of completion, participants must:

  • Attend all sessions (100% attendance required)
  • Actively participate in all exercises and discussions
  • Demonstrate understanding of safety principles
  • Show proper technique in physical skills
  • Complete final scenario assessments
