Craniosacral Therapy Course

Course Overview

Welcome to our comprehensive 3-day certification course in Craniosacral Therapy (CST). This intensive program combines detailed anatomical study with hands-on practice to develop the subtle palpation skills needed for effective craniosacral therapy. Participants will learn to assess and treat the craniosacral system through gentle, non-invasive techniques.

Prerequisites: This course is designed for licensed healthcare professionals, including massage therapists, physical therapists, occupational therapists, osteopaths, chiropractors, nurses, and physicians. Strong foundation in anatomy and physiology, particularly of the nervous system, is required.

Learning Outcomes

Upon completion of this course, participants will be able to:

  • Demonstrate comprehensive understanding of craniosacral system anatomy and physiology
  • Accurately palpate and assess the craniosacral rhythm
  • Perform basic and intermediate craniosacral techniques safely and effectively
  • Apply still point techniques and evaluate their effects
  • Work with the dural membrane system
  • Understand and apply sacral decompression techniques
  • Recognize contraindications and safety considerations
  • Develop appropriate treatment protocols for various conditions

Certification Requirements

  • 100% attendance and participation in all sessions
  • Successful completion of practical assessments
  • Demonstration of proper technique and palpation skills
  • Understanding of contraindications and safety protocols

Note: This foundational certification in Craniosacral Therapy represents the beginning of your CST journey. Advanced courses, continuing education, and regular practice are essential for developing the subtle palpation skills required for mastery. We recommend joining professional CST organizations and seeking mentorship opportunities to further develop your skills.
