Advanced Meditation Techniques - Syllabus

Course Information

Duration: 3 days (21 hours total)
Prerequisites: Established meditation practice, familiarity with basic Buddhist concepts
Location: Sacred practice space with proper shrine room setup

Sacred Traditions Notice

These practices come from sacred lineages and require proper respect and dedication. Participants should:

  • Maintain appropriate conduct and mindfulness
  • Honor the sanctity of transmission lineages
  • Practice with sincere motivation
  • Respect traditional protocols

Required Materials

  • Meditation cushion and mat
  • Practice texts (provided)
  • Journal for notes and experiences
  • Loose, comfortable clothing
  • Ritual items (provided):
    • Chöd drum and bell
    • Singing bowls
    • Mandala materials
    • Sacred art supplies

Practice Objectives

Upon completion, practitioners will be able to:

  • Understand and practice Chöd fundamentals
  • Apply Vijnana Bhairava meditation techniques
  • Perform basic Charya Nritya sacred dance
  • Create and work with mandalas
  • Practice Nine Breathings of Purification
  • Execute Tsa Lung Trul Khor movements
  • Use singing bowls for meditation and healing
  • Maintain proper practice protocols
  • Integrate techniques into daily practice

Practice Guidelines

  • Commitment: Daily practice recommended
  • Progression: Follow traditional sequence
  • Support: Regular guidance available
  • Respect: Honor sacred boundaries

Continuing Practice

After course completion:

  • Connect with practice community
  • Maintain relationship with teachers
  • Attend regular practice sessions
  • Participate in seasonal retreats
  • Access advanced teachings when ready
