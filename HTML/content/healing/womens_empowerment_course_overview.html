Women's Empowerment: Digital Skills, Financial Literacy, and Civic Rights

Course Overview

This three-day empowerment course is designed to provide Nepali women with essential skills and knowledge for personal and professional development. The program focuses on digital literacy, financial management, and understanding civic rights and support systems available in Nepal. All sessions are conducted in both Nepali and English as needed.

Note: This course is open to all women, regardless of educational background. No prior computer or financial experience is required. Support for childcare during course hours can be arranged upon request.
