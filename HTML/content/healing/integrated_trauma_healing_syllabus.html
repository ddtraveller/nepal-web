Integrated Trauma Healing Course - Syllabus

Course Information

Duration: 3 days (21 hours total)
Prerequisites: Mental health professional qualification or equivalent experience
CEU Credits: 21 hours

Important Safety Notice

This course includes experiential components that may activate trauma responses. Participants should:

  • Have access to personal support systems during the training
  • Be prepared to maintain professional boundaries
  • Practice self-care throughout the course
  • Honor personal limits and opt-out options

Required Materials

  • Course manual (provided)
  • EMDR practice equipment (provided)
  • Journaling materials
  • Comfortable clothing for somatic work
  • Blanket and cushion for floor work
  • Traditional healing materials (provided)

Course Objectives

Upon completion, participants will be able to:

  • Understand trauma neurobiology and physiology
  • Apply basic EMDR protocols safely
  • Implement somatic experiencing techniques
  • Practice traditional Nepali healing methods
  • Facilitate collective trauma healing
  • Work with intergenerational trauma
  • Respond effectively to crisis situations
  • Create integrated treatment plans
  • Maintain appropriate therapeutic containers

Assessment Methods

  • Participation (40%)
    • Active engagement in discussions
    • Practice participation
    • Group contributions
  • Practical Skills (40%)
    • Technique demonstration
    • Protocol implementation
    • Safety maintenance
  • Integration Project (20%)
    • Treatment planning
    • Case conceptualization
    • Protocol development

Course Policies

  • Safety: Trauma-informed practices maintained throughout
  • Confidentiality: Strict privacy guidelines observed
  • Support: On-site clinical support available
  • Self-Care: Regular breaks and grounding practices included

Professional Development

Post-course support includes:

  • Monthly consultation group access
  • Online resource library
  • Protocol documentation
  • Professional referral network
  • Continuing education opportunities
