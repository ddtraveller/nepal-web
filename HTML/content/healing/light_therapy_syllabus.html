Light Therapy and Healing Mats Certification Course

Course Information

Duration: 1 day (8 hours)
CEU Credits: 8 hours
Prerequisites: Healthcare or wellness professional certification recommended

Required Materials (Provided)

  • Course manual and handouts
  • Red light therapy devices
  • Blue light therapy devices
  • Various healing mats (PEMF, infrared, etc.)
  • Safety goggles
  • Treatment protocols guide

Safety Guidelines

  • Protective eyewear must be worn during all light therapy demonstrations
  • Proper sanitization protocols must be followed
  • Participants must disclose any photosensitivity conditions
  • Emergency procedures must be reviewed before practical sessions

Course Objectives

Upon completion, participants will be able to:

  • Understand the scientific principles behind light therapy and healing mat technologies
  • Safely operate red and blue light therapy devices
  • Properly use various healing mat technologies
  • Create appropriate treatment protocols for different conditions
  • Identify contraindications and safety considerations
  • Integrate different modalities effectively
  • Maintain proper sanitization and safety standards

Assessment Criteria

  • Practical Skills (50%)
    • Device operation proficiency
    • Safety protocol adherence
    • Treatment application technique
  • Protocol Development (30%)
    • Treatment planning
    • Modality integration
    • Safety considerations
  • Knowledge Assessment (20%)
    • Understanding of principles
    • Safety awareness
    • Contraindication knowledge
