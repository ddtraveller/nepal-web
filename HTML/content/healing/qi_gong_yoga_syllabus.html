Course Description

This intensive three-day course explores the integration of traditional yoga and qigong practices, emphasizing their complementary approaches to mind-body wellness. Students will learn fundamental techniques from both traditions while understanding their historical and philosophical contexts within South and East Asian healing systems.

Course Objectives

Upon successful completion of this course, students will be able to:

  • Demonstrate proper form in fundamental yoga asanas and qigong movements
  • Apply various breathing techniques (pranayama and qigong breathing) effectively
  • Understand the theoretical frameworks of both practices including concepts of prana and qi
  • Develop a personal practice integrating elements from both traditions
  • Apply basic principles of energy work in self-healing practices
  • Articulate the cultural and philosophical contexts of both traditions
