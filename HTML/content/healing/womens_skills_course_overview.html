Practical Life Skills for Nepali Women

Course Overview

This comprehensive three-day course combines traditional Nepali skills with modern practical knowledge. Participants will learn essential sewing techniques, traditional and contemporary cooking methods, home management skills, and practical knowledge for daily life. The course is conducted in Nepali with English support as needed.

Note: This course welcomes women of all skill levels. Basic materials are provided, and participants are encouraged to bring any specific fabrics or ingredients they wish to work with.
