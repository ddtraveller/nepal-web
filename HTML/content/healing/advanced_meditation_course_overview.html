Advanced Meditation Techniques Course

Course Overview

This intensive three-day program offers deep immersion into advanced meditation practices from several sacred traditions. The course combines teachings from Tibetan Chöd practice, Kashmir Shaivism's Vijnana Bhairava Tantra, Newar Buddhist meditation, and Himalayan Bon traditions. Participants will receive authentic instruction in these profound contemplative methods while learning to integrate them into contemporary practice.

Sacred Lineage Notice

These practices come from sacred meditation lineages that have been carefully preserved and transmitted for generations. This course honors these traditions while making their essential teachings accessible to sincere practitioners. Participants are expected to maintain appropriate respect and dedication throughout the training.

Prerequisites: This course is designed for experienced meditators with:

  • Established daily meditation practice (minimum 1 year)
  • Basic understanding of Buddhist concepts
  • Previous retreat experience recommended
  • Sincere commitment to practice
