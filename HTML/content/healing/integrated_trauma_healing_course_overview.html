Integrated Trauma Healing Course

Course Overview

This intensive three-day training program combines modern neuroscience-based approaches with traditional healing practices to provide a comprehensive framework for trauma healing. The course integrates EMDR, Somatic Experiencing, traditional Nepali healing practices, and other modalities to address individual, collective, and intergenerational trauma. Participants will develop practical skills while gaining deep theoretical understanding of trauma's impact on mind, body, and spirit.

Important Safety Notice

This course includes experiential components that may activate trauma responses. We maintain strict safety protocols and provide ongoing support throughout the training. Participants should have established self-care practices and access to support systems during the course.

Prerequisites: This course is designed for mental health professionals, healthcare providers, and practitioners working with trauma. Basic understanding of trauma-informed care principles is recommended.
