Polyvagal Theory in Clinical Practice - Syllabus

Course Information

Duration: 3 days (21 hours total)
Prerequisites: Healthcare or mental health professional qualification recommended
CEU Credits: 21 hours

Required Materials

  • Course manual (provided)
  • Workbook for exercises (provided)
  • Anatomical charts (provided)
  • Note-taking materials
  • Comfortable clothing for practical exercises
  • Yoga mat or blanket for floor work

Course Objectives

Upon completion, participants will be able to:

  • Understand the fundamental principles of Polyvagal Theory
  • Identify and assess autonomic states in clients
  • Apply appropriate interventions for different autonomic states
  • Utilize the Social Engagement System therapeutically
  • Implement trauma-informed polyvagal approaches
  • Develop effective treatment plans using polyvagal principles
  • Practice personal regulation strategies
  • Lead polyvagal-informed group interventions
