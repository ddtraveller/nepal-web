Light Therapy and Healing Mats Course

Course Overview

This intensive one-day certification course provides comprehensive training in the application of red light therapy, blue light therapy, and healing mat technologies. Participants will gain both theoretical knowledge and practical skills necessary for safe and effective treatment delivery. The course combines lectures, demonstrations, and hands-on practice to ensure competency in these cutting-edge therapeutic modalities.

Prerequisites: This course is designed for healthcare and wellness professionals. While not mandatory, prior experience in therapeutic modalities is recommended. Basic knowledge of anatomy and physiology is beneficial.
