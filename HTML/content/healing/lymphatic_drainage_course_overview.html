Manual Lymphatic Drainage and Complete Lymphedema Decongestive Therapy Course

Course Overview

Welcome to our comprehensive 3-day certification course in Manual Lymphatic Drainage (MLD) and Complete Lymphedema Decongestive (CLD) Therapy. This intensive program combines theory and hands-on practice to equip healthcare professionals with the skills needed to perform these essential therapeutic techniques and manage lymphedema effectively.

Prerequisites: This course is designed for licensed healthcare professionals, including massage therapists, physical therapists, occupational therapists, nurses, and physicians. Basic anatomy and physiology knowledge is required.

Learning Outcomes

Upon completion of this course, participants will be able to:

  • Demonstrate comprehensive understanding of lymphatic system anatomy and physiology
  • Perform complete MLD sequences for face, neck, upper extremities, trunk, and lower extremities
  • Conduct thorough lymphedema assessments and measurements
  • Perform multi-layer compression bandaging for lymphedema management
  • Teach appropriate lymphedema exercises and self-management techniques
  • Implement comprehensive CLD treatment protocols
  • Document treatments and track client progress effectively

Certification Requirements

  • 100% attendance and participation in all sessions
  • Successful completion of practical assessments
  • Demonstration of proper technique and safety protocols
  • Understanding of contraindications and precautions

Note: This certification provides the foundation for practicing MLD and CLD therapy. Continuing education and practice are recommended for maintaining and advancing skills. Additionally, some jurisdictions may require specific licensing for lymphedema therapy; please check your local requirements.
