Women's Safety and Crisis Response Course - Syllabus

Course Information

Duration: 1 day (7 hours)
Language: Nepali and English
Location: Secure community center

Confidentiality and Safety Notice

  • All information shared remains strictly confidential
  • Private, secure location for all sessions
  • Support staff available throughout the course
  • Counselors present for emotional support
  • Safe transportation assistance available

Course Materials

  • Safety planning workbook
  • Emergency contact cards
  • Legal rights handbook
  • Resource directory
  • Document checklist
  • Small notebook for private notes

Morning Session: Immediate Crisis Response and Safety Planning

Time Topic Activity Learning Objectives
9:00am - 9:30am Introduction and Ground Rules Group discussion - Understand confidentiality
- Establish safe space
- Know available support
9:30am - 10:30am Emergency Response Planning Interactive workshop - Create safety signals
- Map escape routes
- Identify safe contacts
10:45am - 11:15am Child Protection Strategies Practical planning - Plan child safety measures
- Teach emergency responses
- Prepare school contacts
11:15am - 12:00pm Evidence Collection Documentation workshop - Learn safe documentation
- Understand legal evidence
- Create secure records

Afternoon Session: Legal Rights and Long-term Planning

Time Topic Activity Learning Objectives
1:00pm - 2:00pm Legal Protections Rights workshop - Know legal rights
- Understand filing processes
- Access legal aid
2:00pm - 2:45pm Financial Security Planning session - Secure essential documents
- Plan financial independence
- Access support resources
3:00pm - 3:45pm Support Networks Resource mapping - Connect with NGOs
- Find safe shelters
- Join support groups
3:45pm - 4:00pm Safety Planning Review Individual consultation - Review personal plans
- Clarify next steps
- Establish support connections

Detailed Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, participants will be able to:

  • Crisis Response
    • Identify warning signs of escalating violence
    • Implement immediate safety strategies
    • Execute emergency exit plans
    • Contact appropriate authorities
  • Child Protection
    • Create child safety plans
    • Prepare emergency contacts for children
    • Understand custody rights
    • Access child support services
  • Legal Knowledge
    • Know their legal rights
    • File police reports effectively
    • Obtain protection orders
    • Access legal aid services
  • Financial Protection
    • Secure important documents
    • Access emergency funds
    • Protect financial assets
    • Plan for financial independence

Support Resources

  • Immediate Crisis Support
    • 24/7 helpline numbers
    • Emergency shelter contacts
    • Police liaison officers
    • Medical support services
  • Legal Resources
    • Women's legal aid organizations
    • Pro-bono lawyer network
    • Court support services
    • Document preparation assistance
  • Long-term Support
    • Counseling services
    • Skills development programs
    • Job placement assistance
    • Housing support services

Safety Guidelines

  • Keep all materials in a secure location
  • Save emergency numbers under code names
  • Clear browser history after accessing resources
  • Use safe addresses for correspondence
  • Trust instincts about safety
  • Have emergency bag ready

Follow-up Support

  • Monthly support group meetings
  • Individual counseling sessions
  • Legal case follow-up
  • Skills training opportunities
  • Ongoing safety planning
