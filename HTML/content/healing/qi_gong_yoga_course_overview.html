Learning Outcomes

Upon completion, participants will be able to:

  • Foundational Practices
    • Perform basic yoga asanas safely
    • Practice fundamental qigong movements
    • Use proper breathing techniques
    • Maintain correct posture and alignment
  • Energy Work
    • Understand basic energy concepts
    • Practice energy cultivation
    • Use healing sounds and mantras
    • Apply self-healing techniques
  • Personal Development
    • Create a daily practice routine
    • Adapt practices for personal needs
    • Use techniques for stress relief
    • Maintain ongoing practice

Practice Guidelines

  • Listen to your body and respect its limits
  • Practice with awareness and intention
  • Maintain regular breathing
  • Keep energy flowing smoothly
  • Stay hydrated throughout practice
  • Rest when needed

Safety Considerations

  • Inform instructor of any health conditions
  • Practice within comfortable limits
  • Use props when needed
  • Take breaks as necessary
  • Stay hydrated
  • Avoid forcing or straining
